# Half Adder
Half adder is a combinational logic circuit that performs the addition of two 1 bit numbers.
> [!NOTICE] 
> Unlike [[Notes/Full Adder\|Full Adder]], the [[Notes/Half Adder\|Half Adder]] does not accept `Cin` at its inputs.
